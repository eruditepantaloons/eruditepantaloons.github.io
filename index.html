Wayfinder HK

A quick and easy way to find essential public facilities for tourists in Hong Kong. Requiring no typing, the app instantly highlights nearby facilities and how to get there. The app also allows users to easily ask for directions in the local language.

Bite Magazine

Bite is a monthly magazine that features works from talented photographers and trendsetting fashion from youth all over the world.

The new Bite magazine website brings the publication's modern aesthetic to the web. The responsive and highly visual design maintains the immersive full page experience of the print magazine, while allowing for multiple screen sizes.
